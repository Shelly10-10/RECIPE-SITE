Chicken Biriyani

Ingredients

  • Basmati Rice
  • Biriyani Masala Powder
  • Fried onion
  • Spices and Herbs
  • Uncooked marinated meat
  • Saffron infused milk

Process

  1. Marinate chicken pieces with yogurt, spices (like turmeric, chili powder, garam masala), and sometimes ginger-garlic paste
  2. Parboil basmati rice with whole spices like cardamom, cloves, and cinnamon. The rice should be only 70-80% cooked to ensure it doesn't get mushy during the final cooking.
  3. Slice and fry onions until golden brown
  4. In a large, heavy-bottomed pan or biryani pot, layer the partially cooked rice over the marinated chicken.
  5. Seal the pot with a tight-fitting lid or dough to trap the steam
  6. Once done, gently mix the layers and garnish with fried onions, mint, and coriander.

Chicken Taco

Ingredients

  • Skinless chicken breasts
  • Olive oil
  • 8 small corn tortillas
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • Garlic and chilli powder
  • Cumin

Process

  1. In a bowl, mix together chili powder, cumin, garlic powder, onion powder, paprika, salt, and pepper.
  2. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium-high heat.
  3. While the chicken is cooking, warm the tortillas in a dry skillet or microwave
  4. Add sliced chicken to the skillet
  5. Assemble the tacos by placing a portion of the cooked chicken on each tortilla.
  6. Add your favorite toppings, such as shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, shredded cheese, salsa, sour cream, and chopped cilantro.

Rasgullas

Ingredients

  • 1 litre full fat milk
  • Lemon juice or white vinegar
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup water

Process

  1. Boil the milk in a heavy-bottomed pan and add lemon juice or vinegar gradually while stirring, until the milk curdles
  2. Once the greenish whey separates from the curd, strain it using a muslin cloth to get chenna (paneer).
  3. Rinse the chenna under cold water to remove any traces of lemon juice or vinegar and tie the chenna in the muslin cloth and hang it for about 30 minutes to drain excess water.
  4. Take the chenna on a clean surface and knead it for about 10-15 minutes until it becomes smooth and soft.
  5. Divide the kneaded chenna into small, smooth balls and flatten them slightly. Make sure there are no cracks
  6. In a wide pan, mix sugar and water and bring it to a boil, stirring until the sugar dissolves completely..
  7. Gently slide the rasgullas into the simmering sugar syrup and cover and cook on medium heat for 15-20 minutes, allowing the rasgullas to puff up and absorb the syrup.

Chilli Garlic Prawns

Ingredients

  • Peeled large prawns
  • Olive oil,cloves and garlics
  • Soya and oyster sauce
  • Red chilli flakes
  • Fresh cilantro
  • Pepper,honey and lemon wedges

Process

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pan over medium-high heat.
  2. Add minced garlic to the hot oil and saut√© for about 30 seconds or until fragrant.
  3. Add the prawns to the pan and cook for 2-3 minutes on each side or until they turn pink and opaque
  4. Sprinkle red chili flakes over the prawns. Adjust the amount based on your spice preference.
  5. Pour the sauce mixture over the prawns in the pan. Toss well to coat the prawns evenly.
  6. Cook for an additional 1-2 minutes, allowing the prawns to absorb the flavors of the sauce.

Chicken Burger

Ingredients

  • Ground chicken
  • Breadcrumbs
  • Spices and onions
  • Tomato and cheese slices
  • Eggs
  • Mustard

Process

  1. In a large bowl, combine ground chicken, breadcrumbs, egg, chopped onion, minced garlic, oregano, thyme, salt, and pepper.
  2. Heat a grill pan or skillet over medium-high heat and cook the chicken patties for about 4-5 minutes per side or until they are cooked through and have a golden brown crust.
  3. Toast the burger buns on the grill or in a toaster.
  4. Spread mayonnaise on the bottom half of the bun, place a lettuce leaf on top of the mayonnaise and add a chicken patty on the lettuce.
  5. Place tomato slices and red onion slices on top of the patty and if you like, add a slice of cheese on top of the hot patty to let it melt.
  6. Spread ketchup and mustard on the top half of the bun.

Ice-Cream

Ingredients

  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon pure vanilla extract

Process

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine the heavy cream and whole milk. Stir until well mixed, add the granulated sugar to the mixture. Continue stirring until the sugar is completely dissolved and mix in the vanilla extract. Ensure an even distribution of all ingredients.
  2. Cover the bowl and place it in the refrigerator for at least 2 hours, or preferably overnight. This allows the mixture to chill and the flavors to meld.
  3. Pour the chilled mixture into the ice cream maker.
  4. Turn on the machine and let it churn for about 20-25 minutes, or until the ice cream reaches a soft-serve consistency.
  5. Smooth the top with a spatula, and then place a piece of parchment paper directly on the surface to prevent ice crystals from forming.
  6. Put the lid on the container and freeze for at least 4 hours or overnight to firm up the ice cream.
